---

## General Online Safety

- [ ] Set all personal social  edia accounts (Instagram, Snapchat, X/Twitter) to **private** and review your followers/friends list regularly.
- [ ] Use a **strong, unique password** for every platform — at least 12 characters with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Never reuse passwords.
- [ ] Enable **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** on every account that supports it (Google, Instagram, GitHub, LinkedIn, etc.).
- [ ] Keep your **device OS and all apps updated** — security patches fix vulnerabilities that attackers exploit.
- [ ] Never click on **unknown or suspicious links** in emails, SMS messages, or social media DMs — even if they appear to come from a known contact.
- [ ] Log out of accounts when using **shared or public computers** (college labs, cafes, libraries).

## Social Media & Communication Safety

- [ ] Think before you post — assume anything shared online can be **screenshotted, forwarded, or resurfaced** at any time in the future.
- [ ] Never share your **home address, daily routine, or real-time location** publicly on social media.
- [ ] If you receive **hostile, threatening, or harassing messages**, do not respond. Screenshot the evidence, then block and report the sender.
- [ ] If you witness cyberbullying of another student, **do not forward or engage** with the content — report it using in-app tools and check in with the victim privately.

## UPI & Financial Safety (India-Specific)

- [ ] **Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or linked bank account details** with anyone — no legitimate bank, government body, or customer support agent will ever ask for these.
- [ ] Before every UPI transaction, **verify the recipient's UPI ID carefully** — scammers use IDs that closely resemble official handles (e.g., `paytm-support@upi` instead of an official ID).
- [ ] Be suspicious of any offer that asks you to **receive money by entering your PIN** — entering your PIN always sends money, never receives it.
- [ ] Only download UPI apps (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm) from the **official Google Play Store or Apple App Store** — never from links sent via SMS or WhatsApp.
